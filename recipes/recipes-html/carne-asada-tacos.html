Carne Asada Tacos

These Carne Asada Tacos are flank steak, Mexican-inspired street tacos!

Just load your tortilla with juicy steak bites, avocado, onion, cotija cheese and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada
  • Tortillas
  • Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Cotija Cheese
  • Avacado
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Grill your steak.
  2. Grab a tortilla and slam some avocado and sour cream into it.
  3. Then add a good portion of carne asada, sprinkle chopped onion, fresh cilantro and cotija cheese in there.
  4. Squeeze some fresh lime on top and BAM! Amazing Carne Asada Tacos every time!
