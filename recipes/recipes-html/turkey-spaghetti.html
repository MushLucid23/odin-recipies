Turkey Spaghetti

The easiest and most delicious Turkey Spaghetti ever!

Using simple ingredients that you have in your fridge/pantry. Done in under 30 minutes. Best spaghetti meat sauce recipe ever.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Seasonings
  • Thin Spaghetti
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Get a Large Saucepan and cooking spoon
  2. Cutting Board and Chefs knife
  3. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside
  4. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add carrot and onion to the pan
  5. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over veggies.
  6. Cover and cook for a few minutes until veggies are tender, stirring occasionlly.
  7. Add garlic and ground turkey to the veggies and cook until turkey is fully cooked through, about 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in tomato sauce, seasonings and parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover and cook over a vey low simmer for another 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Serve Turkey Spaghetti Sauce over cooked spaghetti.
  11. Enjoy!
